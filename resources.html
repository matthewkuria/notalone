Mental Health Resources

Trusted information and support from leading organizations

National Mental Health Organizations

These reputable organizations provide comprehensive mental health information, treatment options, and support services:

NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness)

The nation's largest grassroots mental health organization dedicated to building better lives for Americans affected by mental illness.

Visit Site Helpline

Mental Health America

Leading community-based nonprofit dedicated to addressing the needs of those living with mental illness and promoting mental health.

Visit Site Free Screenings

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones.

Visit Site Call 988

American Psychological Association

Scientific and professional organization representing psychology in the U.S., with excellent mental health resources.

Visit Site Find a Psychologist

National Institute of Mental Health

The lead federal agency for research on mental disorders, offering science-based information.

Visit Site Find Help
