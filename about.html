Our Mission

NeverAlone was founded in 2018 with a simple but powerful goal: to ensure that no one has to face mental health challenges alone. What began as a small peer support group has grown into a thriving community of over 50,000 members across the country.

We believe that connection is healing. By bringing together individuals who understand mental health struggles firsthand, we create safe spaces for sharing, learning, and growing together.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We meet every person where they are with empathy and without judgment.

Community

We believe healing happens in connection with others who understand.

Accessibility

All our resources and support groups are completely free to access.

Hope

We share stories of recovery to inspire hope for everyone's journey.
